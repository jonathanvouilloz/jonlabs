Kidou is a project tracker for indie hackers who want to build in public with a developer-friendly aesthetic.

## The Problem

Most project management tools are either too complex (Jira, Linear) or too casual (Notion, Trello). As a solo builder, I wanted something that:
- Feels like code, not corporate software
- Makes progress visible to others (accountability)
- Extracts structure from my existing docs

## The Solution

A minimal tracker where you:
1. Upload your PRD or project spec
2. AI extracts milestones automatically
3. Check them off as you ship
4. Your progress is public by default

## Why Public?

Building in public creates accountability. When others can see your progress, you ship faster.
