Glana was my attempt at building a project management tool specifically for indie hackers.

## The Idea

Track side projects from idea to launch:
- Capture ideas quickly
- Validate before building
- Track progress with simple milestones
- Ship publicly or keep private

## What I Learned

Built it, used it for a few months, realized I preferred simpler tools like Notion and a plain text file.

## Status

Archived. The code taught me a lot about Next.js and database design, but the product wasn't solving a real problem for me.
