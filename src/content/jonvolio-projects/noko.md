Noko is a lightweight link-in-bio tool I'm building for my clients who need a simple, branded landing page.

## Why Another Linktree?

Most link-in-bio tools are either:
- Bloated with features nobody uses
- Ugly with limited customization
- Expensive for what they offer

## The Approach

Noko focuses on:
- **Speed**: Static generation, no unnecessary JavaScript
- **Simplicity**: Just links, maybe a bio, that's it
- **Customization**: Full control over colors and styling
- **Self-hosted option**: Deploy on your own domain

## Current Status

Building the core features. Will dogfood with a few client projects first before opening up.
